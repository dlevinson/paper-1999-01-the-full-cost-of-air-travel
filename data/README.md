# Data Boundary

This folder contains the paper-specific air-cost data/model materials for:

Gillen, David, and David M. Levinson. (1999). "Full Cost of Air Travel in the California Corridor." Transportation Research Record 1662:1-9. https://doi.org/10.3141/1662-01

The retained data are aggregate/model/statistical materials from the Full Cost Project air-cost work, especially airport movement/enplanement cost workbooks. Very old Mac Excel 5 files are retained as originals because conversion produced blank or duplicate workbooks; string extracts are included under `../documentation/legacy_text_extracts/`.

Repeated Full Cost report, air noise/congestion, and social-cost chart workbooks are not stored here. They are referenced through `_shared_sources/full-cost-intercity-transportation-1996/` and listed in `../data/DEDUP_POINTER_MANIFEST.csv`.
